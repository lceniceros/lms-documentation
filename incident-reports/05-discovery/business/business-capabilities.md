# Business Capabilities Map - Incident

Capabilities grouped by bundle and process.

## By Bundle

- ConfigurationBundle
  - Configure incident types/subtypes
  - Manage form sections and questions
  - Manage company severity levels

- CreationBundle
  - Create incident reports (custom and generic forms)
  - Manage people (involved, injured, witnesses)
  - Handle evidence (file names, deletion)

- TrackingBundle
  - Track incidents and states
  - Escalation via matrix (RACI, levels)
  - Notifications (email), permissions and roles (Voter)
  - Reporting and summaries

## By Process

- Initial Configuration
  - Define incident types, sections, questions, severity

- Report Creation
  - Capture data, participants, and evidence

- Tracking and Escalation
  - Reviews, actions, escalation levels, responsible parties

- Closure and Resolution
  - Resolution info, closure actions, audit trail

> Reference specific controllers and services when expanding each capability.
